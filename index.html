What We're About

Coffee Origin

We source our beans directly from small farms in the Western Ghats, ensuring ethical practices and exceptional flavor profiles. Each batch tells the story of the soil, sun, and care behind it.

Brewing Craft

Every cup is made with intention. Using time-honored pour-over and siphon methods, we highlight the nuanced notes of our hand-roasted beans — from citrus brightness to deep chocolate finish.

Our Space

Located in the heart of Karnal, our café blends warm wood, soft lighting, and quiet corners designed for connection — whether it's your first sip or your daily ritual.
